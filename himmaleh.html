Himmaleh Spirits launches Darma

Himmaleh Spirits has announced the launch of Darma, a premium blended whisky inspired by the Himalayas.

Himmaleh Spirits has announced the launch of Darma, a premium blended whisky inspired by the Himalayas. Crafted with a blend of Indian whisky, select Scottish malts and a subtle bourbon influence, Darma is positioned as a refined expression with a distinctly Himalayan character. Darma premium blended whisky The whisky draws inspiration from the remote Darma Valley in Uttarakhand, reflecting an aura of quiet mystery and layered complexity. In the glass, Darma presents a rich golden hue with a soft amber glow. The aroma opens with toasted notes layered with Madagascan vanilla, hints of coconut and dried fruit. On the palate, it is medium-bodied and smooth, revealing creamy caramel with a gentle trace of spice, followed by a warm, lingering finish. Darma’s packaging mirrors its inspiration, featuring a snow leopard on the label, symbolising balance and mystery, set amid the flora and fauna of the Darma Valley. The sculpted punt at the base of the bottle echoes the contours of the Himalayas.

“The whisky market is growing rapidly and consumers are looking beyond labels; they want authenticity and craftsmanship. With Darma, we saw an opportunity to create a blended whisky that reflects how India is drinking whiskey today. As one of the world’s fastest growing whiskey markets, India is seeing a new generation of drinkers who are curious, informed and looking for quality they can come back to. Darma is our response to that shift; a blended whisky that feels honest, thoughtfully crafted and naturally fits into everyday whisky moments,” said an excited Ansh Khanna, Co-founder, Himmaleh Spirits. Samarth Prasad, Co-Founder of Himmaleh Spirits Co-founder Samarth Prasad adds, “Darma opens a fresh chapter in the Indian whisky story. We wanted a blended whisky with warmth, texture and layered nuance that's still unpretentious and accessible. It’s crafted for everyday enjoyment, whether at home or on holiday, offering premium whisky quality with a relaxed sense of refinement and excellent value for money.” Bottled at 42.8 percent ABV, the whisky is designed to perform well in modern cocktails while remaining equally enjoyable over ice. Darma is available initially in Uttarakhand at a starting price of ₹1,580, followed by launches in Goa and Gurugram, with additional markets to follow.
